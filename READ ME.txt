# FactCheck Pro

A real-time fact-checking iOS application that uses advanced AI and speech recognition to verify statements as they're spoken.

## Features

- **Real-time Speech Recognition**: Continuous audio monitoring and transcription
- **AI-Powered Fact Checking**: Advanced algorithms verify claims against reliable sources
- **Speaker Identification**: Voice recognition and speaker tracking
- **Comprehensive Analytics**: Detailed reporting and statistics
- **Privacy-First Design**: Local processing when possible
- **Multi-language Support**: Support for 8 languages
- **Accessibility**: Full VoiceOver and accessibility support
